Hi!

I'm Zach, a UC Berkeley Student currently studying Civil and Environmental Engineering. I also love taking pictures, camping with friends, and cooking a warm meal (which I can do all at the same time!)

I first started taking pictures on a Nintendo DSi. After beating Pokemon Diamond, I began snapping pictures of Arches National Park with the 0.3 megapixel best of a camera on the DSi. Since then, I've been hooked, and I will always be looking to capture awesome memories from my adventures!

Photography is a way for me to capture experiences and have a great time with lovely people. Through photography, I hope to make everyone look their best, create intriguing art, and capture timeless memories. If you love staring at stars, toasting marshmellos by a fire, or just aimlessly exploring a city, then we'd make great friends!

Let's go shoot sometimes! Contact me and let's talk!
